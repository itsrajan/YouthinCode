About Us
What we do


Programs

We provide coding programs, where we teach different types of languages, such as Python Turtle and Java in an interactive and engaging atmosphere. At the end of the program, students are assigned to create a project, using the code they learned.



Q&A Sessions

By hosting Q&A sessions, Youth in Code members have the opportunity to speak with prominent STEM leaders, who have extensive experience in the field, and get a deeper insight of what it is like working in the STEM field.



Hackathons

We host Hackathons, where students are able to participate in workshops and compete with others with their knowledge in STEM. Youth in Code is hosting one in November, so be on the lookout for that!



Our community

We have currently impacted 5000 students from 5+ countries, and our organization is only growing! Join our slack server to get updated on all of our events and opportunities: Slack Link
