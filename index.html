Our Mission
Learn More About Us!

Women Empowerment-
Focusing on maintaining a 50:50 ratio between male to female within the organization, Youth in Code works to bridge the gender gap by empowering women to get interested and take initiative in computer science.

Collaboration-
Because of the COVID pandemic, it has been getting more difficult for students to receive adequate lessons on computer science. Youth in Code strives to connect students internationally online, giving them the free opportunity to code projects by collaborating and communicating with their peers.

Opportunities-
Youth in Code strongly believes in giving students the access to various opportunities, where they can expand their knowledge of STEM outside of the classroom. We host Q&A sessions, workshops, hackathons, and more.
